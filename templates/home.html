Welcome to House Price Prediction tool! Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, this predictor estimates house prices based on location, ocean proximity, population density, median income, and median age. The model, crafted using the techniques outlined in Aurélien Géron's "Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow" (Second Edition), specifically Chapter 2, utilizes data from the 1990 California census and employs a state-of-the-art RandomForestRegressor. Please note, this project is for demonstration purposes only.
